## Overview

BTRIX Agents are **specialized autonomous AI agents** that work 24/7 inside your BTRIX operating system.

Each agent has a **defined scope**, **specific functions**, and **clear limitations**.

**Important:** Agents are **add-ons** and require an **active BTRIX pack** (Essential, Pro, or Enterprise).

## Agent Activation Rules

### Requirements:
1. **Active BTRIX pack** (Essential, Pro, or Enterprise)
2. **Clear scope definition** (what the agent will do)
3. **Data access** (CRM, analytics, inventory, etc.)

### Permissions by Pack:
- **Essential:** Up to 1 agent recommended
- **Pro:** Up to 3 agents recommended
- **Enterprise:** 2-6 agents included, more available

---

## Agent Audit & Logs

All agents maintain:
- **Action logs** (what they did)
- **Reasoning logs** (why they did it)
- **Fallback triggers** (when to escalate to humans)

Logs are available in the BTRIX dashboard.
