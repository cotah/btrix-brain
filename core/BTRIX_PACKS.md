## Overview

BTRIX operates with **three main service packs** (also called **plans**, **packages**, or **tiers**), each designed for different business sizes and automation needs.

**Pricing structure:** All packs have a **setup fee** (one-time) + **monthly subscription** (recurring).

**Common terms:** cost, price, monthly fee, setup cost, subscription, payment, investment, pricing, rates.

All packs include:
- Initial setup and configuration
- Channel integration (WhatsApp, website, email, etc.)
- Supabase database setup
- 24/7 AI support
- Monthly recurring operation

## Upgrade Path

Clients can upgrade at any time:

- **Essential → Pro:** Pay setup difference (€800) + new monthly rate
- **Pro → Enterprise:** Custom quote based on requirements

Downgrades are not typically offered due to operational complexity.

---

## Important Notes

1. **All prices are in EUR (€)**
2. **Setup fee is one-time only**
3. **Monthly fees are recurring (MRR)**
4. **Minimum contract:** 6 months
5. **AI Agents require an active pack**
6. **Custom quotes for Enterprise are based on:**
   - Number of channels
   - Integration complexity
   - Number of agents
   - Volume of operations
